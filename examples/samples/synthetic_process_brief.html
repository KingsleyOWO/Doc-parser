Demo Document Intake Process

This synthetic process brief is designed to demonstrate headings, step-like layout blocks, and owner tables.

1. UploadOperator uploads a PDF, Office file, image, or HTML document.
2. ParseParser extracts text, tables, images, and page-level layout data.
3. EnrichOptional VLM summarizes forms, figures, and complex visual blocks.
4. PackageOutputs are saved as Markdown, TXT, DOCX, chunks, and source maps.
